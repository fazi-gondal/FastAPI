# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 2, 2026\
**Last Updated**: January 2, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Social Media Downloader ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

* **Video URLs**: URLs you paste into the app to download videos
* **Device Storage**: Videos are saved to your device's local storage/gallery

### Automatically Collected Information

* **Device Information**: Operating system version, device model (for compatibility)
* **Usage Data**: App performance metrics, crash reports (anonymous)
* **Network Information**: Internet connectivity status (to ensure downloads work)

## What We DO NOT Collect

❌ We **DO NOT** collect:

* Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
* Login credentials or passwords
* Social media account information
* Payment information
* Location data
* Contacts or photos (except what you explicitly download)

## How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

* ✅ Provide video downloading functionality
* ✅ Improve app performance and fix bugs
* ✅ Ensure compatibility with your device
* ✅ Display download progress and status

## Data Storage

* **Local Storage Only**: All downloaded videos are stored locally on your device
* **No Cloud Storage**: We do not upload, store, or backup your downloads to any server
* **Temporary Server Use**: When downloading, videos may temporarily pass through our server but are immediately deleted after transfer
* **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

Our app connects to:

* **Social Media Platforms**: To retrieve video metadata and download links (e.g., Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
* **Content Delivery Networks**: These platforms may have their own privacy policies

We do not control these third-party services and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

* ✅ HTTPS encryption for all network communications
* ✅ No permanent server-side storage of user data
* ✅ Regular security updates

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

* **Access**: View what limited data we collect
* **Delete**: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
* **Control**: Manage app permissions in your device settings

## Data Retention

* **Downloaded Videos**: Stored on your device until you delete them
* **App Usage Data**: Anonymized and retained for up to 90 days
* **Server Logs**: Automatically deleted within 24 hours
